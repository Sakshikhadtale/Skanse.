CATERING

Our goal is to provide world class catering service to our clients.

We ensure the high quality of the food we serve and employ highly qualiﬁed chefs and cooks, supported by a well-trained kitchen and dining crews.

We are experts when it comes to hospitality, industrial and institutional catering. We specialize in banquets, luncheons and catering for sporting and major events. We constantly push ourselves to work hard in order to deliver the best dining experience to our guests.

What we do

Facility management & support services

We also provide support when it comes to managing the needs of our clients’ facilities, whether it be a warehouse, an office, a building or a campsite.

We help in the day-to-day responsibilities of running the facility by providing valuable assistance in the following areas: camp management, waste management, maintenance, car wash, transportation, landscaping, warehouse management and supply of foodstuff & materials.
