DAMAC PROPERTIES

DAMAC Properties has been at the forefront of the Middle East’s luxury real estate market since 2002, delivering residential, commercial and leisure properties across the region, including the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Jordan, Lebanon and the United Kingdom.

Making its mark at the highest end of stylish living, DAMAC Properties has cemented its place as the leading luxury developer in the region, having delivered 36,400 homes, with a development portfolio of 29,000 units at various stages of progress. As of Sept, 30, 2021.

DAMAC Tower Nine Elms, London

An exclusive partnership with Versace Home, and a striking new addition to the London skyline, DAMAC Tower Nine Elms is a 50-storey development with one, two and three bedroom luxury apartments.

Spanning 4 million square feet, featuring world-class amenities and 8,010 square feet of communal outdoor space, the development offers exterior views embellished by the Thames and other iconic landmarks in London city.

801 The Queensway, Toronto, Canada

801 The Queensway is an urban, design-forward mid-rise development by DAMAC Group built in a Joint Venture with Toronto-based Marlin Spring.

Located southwest of Toronto’s downtown core in the south Etobicoke neighbourhood, this mixeduse condominium development will feature both commercial space and residential units.

  • Prime location southwest of downtown across from Queensway Park
  • Close proximity to the Gardiner Expressway and Lakeshore Boulevard
  • Easy connectivity through the Toronto Transit Commission (TTC) bus route
